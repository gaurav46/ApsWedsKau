Learning to become a Professional
This course is designed mainly for those who want to pursue their career in music as a professional musician. The courses are taught according to Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM) and Trinity College of Music standards.

The main modules and training methods are as follows

  - Learn Electronic Keyboard Courses
     > Basic Electronic Keyboard Course
     > Graded Electronic Keyboard Course
     > Computer Music Course
     > Indian Music Course

  - Learn Guitar Courses
     > Basic Guitar Course
     > Graded Guitar Course
     > Acoustic Guitar Course
     > Classical Guitar Course
     > Flamenco Guitar Course
     > 12 String Guitar Course
     > Electric & Bass Guitar Course

Learning For Fun
There are variety of Music courses designed for a person who wishes to learn music in a quick way. Basically you will be taught the simple theory of music skipping away the tough notations which are not required for a person to be a fun learner. You will be able to play music in no time since the beginning of this course.

This course has been mainly designed for person who has attitude to sing, but don't know how to play an instrument. For you, This is the right course. By knowing the basic tactics of the music you can thrill your friends and show your talent. We offer Courses in...

  - Acoustic and Electric Guitar for leisure and fun
  - Keyboards for Leisure and fun

You can choose your interest in any of the above courses. Please contact us for more details.
